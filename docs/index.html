A high-AUC churn classifier still cannot answer whether discounting a specific customer will change their behaviour. Predictive accuracy and causal validity are orthogonal. Double ML residualizes both outcome and treatment on the confounders, then regresses the residuals — isolating the causal signal without requiring a randomized experiment.

Causal identification — refutation tests

Placebo treatment

Shuffled treatment column: effect collapsed from -8.3pp to +0.5pp. The estimator was genuinely using the treatment column, not spurious correlation.

Data subset

80% random subsamples: -8.6 ± 0.7pp. Stable sign and magnitude across 8 resamplings — not a fluke of the full sample.

Random confounder

Added pure-noise feature to the confounder set: estimate moved to -8.4pp. No overfitting to spurious structure.
